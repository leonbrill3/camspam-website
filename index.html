How it works

Built-in camera for temporary photos

CamSpam has its own camera so you can take photos that never touch your camera roll in the first place. Taking a photo of a receipt? A whiteboard? A parking spot? Shoot it with CamSpam and it goes straight to the gallery with an auto-delete timer. Add the home screen widget for one-tap access.

AI finds the junk

CamSpam automatically scans your camera roll and identifies photos that look like spam — screenshots, documents, receipts, QR codes, and other temporary photos. Detected photos are safely moved to the CamSpam gallery for review. Nothing touches your originals until you decide.

Organized and searchable

Detected photos are safely stored in the CamSpam gallery, organized into folders — Receipts, Screenshots, Documents, and more. Search for anything instantly. Each photo shows a countdown timer. Tap any photo to rescue it back to your camera roll, or let it expire. You're always in control.
