What is CamSpam?

Your camera roll is full of photos you'll never look at again. Screenshots you took once. Receipts for returns. Notes from whiteboards. Photos of parking spots. That's camera spam.

CamSpam scans your camera roll with on-device AI and identifies these throwaway photos. Nothing is ever deleted without your approval. Detected photos are moved to the CamSpam gallery where you can review them, rescue anything you want to keep, and let the rest expire.

Your photos are always safe. All processing happens on your iPhone. Your photos never leave your device. No cloud, no servers, no data collection.

How it works

AI finds the junk

CamSpam automatically scans your camera roll and identifies photos that look like spam — screenshots, documents, receipts, QR codes, and other temporary photos. Detected photos are safely moved to the CamSpam gallery for review. Nothing touches your originals until you decide.

Review in your own time

Detected photos are safely stored in the CamSpam gallery, organized into folders — Receipts, Screenshots, Documents, and more. Each photo shows a countdown timer. Tap any photo to rescue it back to your camera roll, or let it expire. You're always in control.

Powerful features

Auto AI Detection scans your camera roll automatically. Auto Cam Roll Cleaner removes expired photos. Auto-Sort Folders keeps everything organized. Add a home screen widget to capture temporary photos that auto-delete.
