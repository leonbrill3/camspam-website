Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

CamSpam ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Photos and Camera Data: CamSpam accesses your device's camera and photo library to provide its core functionality. This includes:

  • Photos you capture using the CamSpam camera
  • Photos from your camera roll that you choose to scan for cleanup
  • Image metadata (such as date taken) to organize and categorize photos

On-Device Processing: All photo analysis and AI processing happens entirely on your device. Your photos are never uploaded to our servers or any third-party servers.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we access solely to:

  • Analyze photos on your device to identify potential spam or temporary photos
  • Organize photos into categories (screenshots, receipts, documents, etc.)
  • Enable search functionality within the app
  • Manage photo expiration timers you set

Data Storage

Local Storage Only: All photos managed by CamSpam are stored locally on your device. We do not upload, transmit, or store your photos on any external servers.

No Cloud Sync: CamSpam does not sync your photos to cloud services. Your photos remain on your device unless you explicitly choose to export or share them.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information or photos to third parties. Your photos and data remain private and under your control at all times.

Analytics

We may collect anonymous usage analytics to improve the app, such as:

  • App crash reports
  • Feature usage statistics (not linked to photos or personal data)
  • Device type and iOS version

This data is anonymized and cannot be used to identify you or access your photos.

Third-Party Services

CamSpam may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple's Core ML: For on-device image analysis (no data leaves your device)
  • Sentry: For crash reporting and error tracking (no photo data is shared)
  • AppsFlyer: For anonymous install attribution analytics

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke camera and photo library permissions at any time through iOS Settings
  • Delete all data by uninstalling the app
  • Request information about what data we process

Children's Privacy

CamSpam is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data. Since all photo processing occurs on-device and we do not transmit your photos, your data benefits from iOS's built-in security features including device encryption.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us at:

support@camspam.com

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Since we do not sell personal information and process photos only on-device, most CCPA requirements are inherently satisfied by our privacy-first design.

International Users

CamSpam is operated from the United States. If you are accessing the app from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be processed in the United States. By using the app, you consent to this processing.
